How to Make The Perfect Omelete

Omelette is Nice, Quick, Easy and my Go-To Breakfast

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/4 tbs Oil
  • 1 Pan
  • 1 Scoop Of Butter

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. First Take A Pan and heat it on low heat for 1.5 min
  2. Then, Add 1/4 tbs of Oil
  3. And then, Add 1 Scoop of butter which would acts as a Lubricant
  4. Heat it for a bit
  5. While The pan is getting hetaed, Whisk 2 Eggs until it is Fluffy And Smooth
  6. Pour The Whisked Eggs into the Pan
  7. Let it Simmer For a Bit until it starts to get seome bubbles
  8. Flip the Eggs so it cooks evenly from both sides
  9. Sprinkle A bit of Salt and Pepper to give it a taste
  10. And Voila, Its youur simple, quick and elicious Omelette to Fill YOUR Tummy
